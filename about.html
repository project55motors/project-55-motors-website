About Us

At Project 55 Motors in South Shropshire, we believe buying a car should be simple, honest and enjoyable.

We source low-mileage vehicles with strong service records, taking the time to ensure each one meets our standards.

We specialise in low-mileage, well-documented cars that we would happily own ourselves. Every vehicle receives a thorough mechanical inspection, professional valet and HPI clearance before sale.
